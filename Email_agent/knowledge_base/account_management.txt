NexusFlow Account Management Guide

Account Types:

1. Free Plan
- Up to 5 workflows
- 1,000 executions per month
- Basic connectors
- Community support
- Email support (48-hour response)

2. Professional Plan
- Unlimited workflows
- 50,000 executions per month
- All connectors
- Priority email support (24-hour response)
- Advanced analytics
- Custom integrations

3. Enterprise Plan
- Unlimited workflows and executions
- All features included
- Dedicated account manager
- 24/7 phone and email support
- Custom SLA
- On-premise deployment option
- Single Sign-On (SSO)
- Advanced security features

Account Settings:

Password Management:
- Passwords must be at least 12 characters
- Must include uppercase, lowercase, numbers, and special characters
- Password reset: Go to Settings > Security > Change Password
- Forgot password: Click "Forgot Password" on login page, check email for reset link

Two-Factor Authentication (2FA):
- Recommended for all accounts
- Enable in Settings > Security > Two-Factor Authentication
- Supports authenticator apps (Google Authenticator, Authy)
- Backup codes provided during setup

API Key Management:
- Generate keys in Settings > API Keys
- Each key can have specific permissions
- Keys can be revoked at any time
- Last 4 characters of key are visible for identification
- Full key shown only once at creation

Team Management:
- Add team members in Settings > Team
- Assign roles: Admin, Developer, Viewer
- Admin: Full access to all settings
- Developer: Can create and modify workflows
- Viewer: Read-only access

Billing and Subscriptions:

Payment Methods:
- Credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express)
- ACH/Bank transfer (Enterprise only)
- Annual billing available with 15% discount

Billing Cycle:
- Monthly: Charged on the same date each month
- Annual: Charged once per year, 15% discount applied

Upgrade/Downgrade:
- Upgrade: Immediate access to new features
- Downgrade: Takes effect at end of current billing period
- Prorated credits applied for downgrades

Invoice Access:
- View invoices in Settings > Billing > Invoices
- Download PDF invoices
- Email invoices to accounting team

Trial Period:
- 14-day free trial of Professional plan
- No credit card required
- Full access to all Professional features
- Automatic conversion to Free plan after trial ends
- Can upgrade anytime during or after trial

Account Limits:

Workflow Limits:
- Free: 5 workflows maximum
- Professional: Unlimited
- Enterprise: Unlimited

Execution Limits:
- Free: 1,000 executions/month
- Professional: 50,000 executions/month
- Enterprise: Unlimited

Storage Limits:
- Free: 1 GB
- Professional: 100 GB
- Enterprise: Unlimited

Data Retention:
- Free: 30 days
- Professional: 1 year
- Enterprise: Unlimited

Account Deletion:
- Request deletion in Settings > Account > Delete Account
- 30-day grace period before permanent deletion
- All data permanently deleted after grace period
- Cannot be undone

Data Export:
- Export all data before deletion
- Available in Settings > Data Export
- Includes workflows, execution history, and logs
- Export format: JSON
